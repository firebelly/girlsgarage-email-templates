8 projects, 100 girls, ONE EPIC SUMMER!

That's a wrap on our 7th year of Girls Garage summer camp! In our most ambitious summer yet, we welcomed 100 girls and built 8 projects for community clients over 6 weeks of camps. ALL of our teen girls attended at no cost

We Built/Made

2 sandboxes and 2 kinetic wind sculptures

for Franklin Preschool and Hopkins Preschool in Berkeley

An 8x20 foot public parklet bench

outside Sanctuary Bistro

3 mini houses

for ReBuilding Together’s San Francisco chapter, which they will be using at their 30th anniversary party as photo booths and informational kiosks

150 block-printed postcards

in response to the treatment of children at the US/Mexico border, which we sent to 3 of our senators and representatives

To all of the girls and families who joined us this summer, THANK YOU from the bottom of our toolbelts. You are our Girls Garage family and community, and your commitment to supporting the next generation of fearless builder girls is the reason we exist.

An extra special thanks goes out to our #Fearless100 supporters, who donated $100 or more in order to make our summer programming FREE for all teen girls, and affordable for all families through full and partial scholarships.

And lastly, we thank our incredible funders and partners who made this summer's programming possible:
